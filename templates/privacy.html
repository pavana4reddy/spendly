Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the Service, we collect the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

We do not collect payment information, government identifiers, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary to provide the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate and improve Spendly. This includes displaying your expense history, generating spending summaries, and maintaining your account.

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone outside of what is required to run the Service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This may cause your browser to make a request to Google's servers. We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-broker services.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any external sites or services that may be linked from the app.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on the app. We will respond as soon as reasonably possible.
